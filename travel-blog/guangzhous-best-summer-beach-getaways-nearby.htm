Guangzhou’s Best Summer Beach Getaways Nearby

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

When the summer heat in Guangzhou becomes unbearable, there’s no better escape than a quick beach getaway. While Guangzhou itself isn’t a coastal city, its proximity to some stunning beaches makes it an ideal hub for sun-seekers. Whether you’re looking for soft white sands, crystal-clear waters, or vibrant beachside activities, these nearby destinations offer the perfect summer retreat.

Top Beach Destinations Within 3 Hours from Guangzhou

1. Xichong Beach (Dapeng Peninsula, Shenzhen)

Just a 2.5-hour drive from Guangzhou, Xichong Beach is a hidden gem on Shenzhen’s Dapeng Peninsula. Known for its pristine coastline and turquoise waters, this beach is perfect for those who crave a quieter, more natural setting.

Why Visit Xichong?

  • Untouched Beauty: Unlike more commercialized beaches, Xichong remains relatively undeveloped, offering a serene escape.
  • Water Sports Paradise: Surfing, paddleboarding, and snorkeling are popular here, with rental shops available.
  • Camping Under the Stars: Many visitors opt for overnight stays in tents, enjoying bonfires and stargazing.

Pro Tip: Visit on weekdays to avoid crowds, as weekends can get busy with Shenzhen locals.

2. Dameisha and Xiaomeisha Beaches (Shenzhen)

If you prefer a livelier atmosphere, Dameisha and Xiaomeisha in Shenzhen are excellent choices. These beaches are well-equipped with amenities, making them great for families.

What Makes Them Special?

  • Free Entry: Dameisha is one of the few free public beaches in the area.
  • Water Parks & Activities: Both beaches feature water slides, jet skiing, and beach volleyball.
  • Scenic Coastal Walks: The promenade connecting Dameisha and Xiaomeisha offers breathtaking ocean views.

Best Time to Go: Early mornings or late afternoons to avoid peak sun hours.

3. Yangmeikeng Beach (Shenzhen)

For those who love dramatic coastal landscapes, Yangmeikeng Beach is a must-visit. Located on the eastern side of Dapeng Peninsula, this beach is famous for its rugged cliffs and clear waters.

Highlights of Yangmeikong

  • Photography Hotspot: The rocky outcrops and crashing waves create stunning backdrops.
  • Seafood Feasts: Nearby fishing villages serve fresh catches at affordable prices.
  • Hiking Trails: Explore the surrounding hills for panoramic ocean views.

Note: The beach has an entrance fee, but the scenery is well worth it.

Hidden Gems Beyond Shenzhen

4. Hailing Island (Yangjiang)

About a 3.5-hour drive from Guangzhou, Hailing Island is a lesser-known paradise with golden sands and gentle waves.

Why Hailing Island?

  • Long, Sandy Shores: The beach stretches for miles, offering plenty of space to relax.
  • Fishing Village Charm: Explore local culture in nearby Shanpo Village.
  • Affordable Resorts: Budget-friendly accommodations line the coast.

Best Activity: Try windsurfing or simply enjoy a sunset stroll.

5. Zhuhai’s Golden Beaches

Zhuhai, often called the "City of Islands," boasts several beautiful beaches just 2 hours from Guangzhou.

Top Picks in Zhuhai

  • Jinwan Beach: Known for its soft sand and calm waters, ideal for swimming.
  • Feisha Beach: A quieter option with fewer tourists.
  • Wailingding Island: A short ferry ride away, offering secluded coves and snorkeling spots.

Bonus: Combine your beach day with a visit to Chimelong Ocean Kingdom for a full day of fun.

Beachside Eats & Local Delicacies

No beach trip is complete without indulging in fresh seafood and tropical drinks. Here are some must-try dishes near these destinations:

  • Grilled Oysters (Shenzhen): A coastal staple, best enjoyed with garlic and chili.
  • Yangjiang Seafood Hotpot: Fresh catches cooked in a flavorful broth.
  • Zhuhai’s Coconut Chicken Soup: A refreshing post-beach meal.

Travel Tips for a Smooth Beach Escape

  • Pack Light but Smart: Sunscreen, hats, and reusable water bottles are essentials.
  • Check Weather & Tides: Avoid visiting during typhoon season (July-September).
  • Book in Advance: Weekend accommodations fill up quickly, especially in Shenzhen.

Whether you’re craving adventure or relaxation, these nearby beaches offer the perfect summer escape from Guangzhou’s hustle and bustle. So grab your swimsuit, hit the road, and dive into the ultimate coastal retreat!
