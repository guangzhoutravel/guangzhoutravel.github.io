Guangzhou’s Best Riverfront Restaurants with a View

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Guangzhou, a bustling metropolis in southern China, is famous for its vibrant food scene and stunning riverfront views. The Pearl River (Zhujiang) winds through the city, offering a picturesque backdrop for some of the best dining experiences in town. Whether you’re a traveler looking for a memorable meal or a local seeking a new spot to unwind, these riverfront restaurants combine delicious cuisine with breathtaking scenery.

Fine Dining with a Riverside Ambiance

Canton Place

Overlooking the Pearl River near Tianzi Wharf, Canton Place is a top choice for those who appreciate Cantonese cuisine with a modern twist. The restaurant’s floor-to-ceiling windows provide an unobstructed view of the river, especially enchanting at night when the city lights reflect on the water. Signature dishes like roasted goose and steamed fish are must-tries, paired perfectly with their curated selection of teas.

The Riverside Grill

If you’re craving Western flavors, The Riverside Grill offers premium steaks and seafood with a side of stunning river views. Located in the heart of the Zhujiang New Town district, this upscale spot is ideal for a romantic dinner. Their dry-aged ribeye and lobster thermidor are crowd favorites, best enjoyed on the outdoor terrace as boats glide by.

Casual Eats by the Water

Shamian Island Café

Nestled on historic Shamian Island, this charming café serves light bites and artisanal coffee with a view of the river. The colonial-era architecture and tree-lined streets add to the relaxed vibe, making it a perfect stop for brunch or an afternoon tea break. Try their avocado toast or freshly baked croissants while watching the river traffic drift past.

Pearl River Beer Garden

For a more laid-back experience, head to the Pearl River Beer Garden near Haixinsha. This open-air venue is a hotspot for craft beer lovers, offering local brews and international favorites. The casual menu features grilled skewers, burgers, and crispy fries—ideal for sharing with friends as you enjoy the evening breeze off the river.

Rooftop Bars & Lounges

Sky Lounge at the Four Seasons

Perched high above the Pearl River, the Sky Lounge at the Four Seasons Guangzhou is the ultimate destination for cocktails with a view. The infinity pool and sleek modern design create a luxurious atmosphere, while the mixologists craft inventive drinks like the "Pearl River Sunset" (a blend of rum, mango, and passionfruit).

VUE Bar at the Hyatt

Another rooftop gem, VUE Bar offers panoramic views of the river and the iconic Canton Tower. Their small plates—think truffle fries and sushi rolls—pair well with their signature mojitos. Arrive before sunset to snag a prime spot by the railing for the best photo opportunities.

Hidden Gems Along the River

The Secret Garden

Tucked away in a quieter stretch of the riverbank, The Secret Garden is a quaint restaurant specializing in fusion cuisine. The outdoor seating is surrounded by lush greenery, creating a serene escape from the city’s hustle. Their lemongrass-marinated grilled prawns and coconut curry are standout dishes.

Fisherman’s Wharf

A local favorite, Fisherman’s Wharf serves fresh seafood right by the water. The no-frills, rustic setting lets the food—and the view—shine. Order the chili crab or steamed clams, and don’t forget to try their homemade chili sauce.

Tips for Dining by the Pearl River

  • Timing is Key: Sunset and evening hours offer the best views, but reservations are recommended for popular spots.
  • Dress Code: While some places are casual, fine-dining venues may require smart attire.
  • Transport: Many riverfront restaurants are accessible via the Guangzhou Metro, but ride-hailing apps like Didi can be convenient for late-night returns.

Whether you’re in the mood for gourmet Cantonese dishes, international flavors, or just a cold drink with a view, Guangzhou’s riverfront dining scene has something for everyone. The combination of great food and stunning scenery makes these spots unforgettable.
