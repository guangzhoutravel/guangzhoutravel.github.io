Guangzhou to Zhuhai: Best Travel Journals & Planners

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Traveling from Guangzhou to Zhuhai is an adventure filled with vibrant cityscapes, coastal charm, and cultural gems. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a family, this route offers endless possibilities for exploration. Below, we dive into the best travel journals, planners, and must-see spots to make your journey unforgettable.

Why Travel from Guangzhou to Zhuhai?

Guangzhou and Zhuhai are two of Guangdong Province’s most dynamic cities. Guangzhou, a bustling metropolis, contrasts beautifully with Zhuhai’s relaxed seaside vibe. The journey between them is seamless, thanks to high-speed trains, buses, and ferries. Here’s why this trip should be on your bucket list:

  • Scenic Diversity: From Guangzhou’s skyscrapers to Zhuhai’s beaches.
  • Cultural Fusion: Experience Cantonese heritage and modern attractions.
  • Food Paradise: Savor dim sum, seafood, and local delicacies.

Best Transportation Options

High-Speed Train (Guangzhou South Station to Zhuhai Station)

The fastest way to travel (approx. 1 hour). Tickets cost around ¥70-¥120. Pro tip: Book in advance during holidays.

Intercity Buses

Affordable but slower (2.5-3 hours). Ideal for budget travelers.

Ferries from Nansha or Panyu

A scenic route (90 minutes) with views of the Pearl River Delta.

Must-Visit Stops Along the Way

Shunde (顺德)

A foodie’s dream! Famous for its double-skin milk (双皮奶) and steamed milk pudding. Don’t miss:
- Qinghui Garden – A classical Lingnan-style garden.
- Shunfeng Mountain Park – Perfect for a quick hike.

Zhongshan (中山)

Birthplace of Dr. Sun Yat-sen. Highlights:
- Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall – A tribute to China’s revolutionary leader.
- Xiaolan Chrysanthemum Festival (if visiting in November).

Zhuhai’s Top Attractions

Chimelong Ocean Kingdom

One of the world’s best aquariums. Must-see:
- Whale shark exhibits
- Thrilling roller coasters
- Nighttime fireworks & shows

Lover’s Road (情侣路)

A romantic coastal walk with stunning views of the Zhuhai Fisher Girl statue and Macau skyline.

Hengqin Island

Home to Chimelong resorts and the Hengqin Port, a gateway to Macau.

Hidden Gems & Local Experiences

Qi’ao Island (淇澳岛)

A quiet escape with:
- Mangrove forests
- Ancient temples
- Fresh seafood shacks

Beishan Village (北山村)

A hipster’s paradise with:
- Art galleries
- Cafés in renovated heritage houses
- Live music at Old Man Club

Best Time to Visit

  • Spring (March-May): Pleasant weather, fewer crowds.
  • Autumn (October-November): Ideal for outdoor activities.
  • Avoid summer typhoons and Golden Week (October 1-7) if you dislike crowds.

Travel Journals & Planners

Recommended Apps & Tools

  • Trip.com: For booking trains and hotels.
  • Didi (滴滴): Reliable ride-hailing in both cities.
  • WeChat Mini Programs: Check real-time ferry schedules.

Sample 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Guangzhou to Zhuhai

  • Morning: Take the high-speed train.
  • Afternoon: Explore Lover’s Road and check into a seaside hotel.
  • Evening: Dinner at Gongbei Underground Market for street food.

Day 2: Chimelong & Hengqin

  • Full day at Chimelong Ocean Kingdom.
  • Optional: Cross to Macau via Hengqin Port.

Day 3: Qi’ao Island & Departure

  • Morning: Bike tour of Qi’ao Island.
  • Afternoon: Return to Guangzhou or extend your stay.

Foodie’s Guide

Guangzhou Must-Eats

  • Dim Sum at **Lian Xiang Lou (莲香楼)
  • Roast Goose at **Yue Kee (裕记)

Zhuhai’s Seafood Delights

  • Gongbei Seafood Street – Try steamed scallops and spicy crab.
  • Jida Night Market – For grilled oysters and zháliáng (炸两).

Budget Tips

  • Use public bikes in Zhuhai for cheap coastal rides.
  • Stay in hostels or budget hotels near Gongbei for affordability.
  • Buy combo tickets for Chimelong to save.

Final Travel Hacks

  • Language: Learn basic Cantonese phrases or use translation apps.
  • Cash vs. Mobile Pay: Most places accept WeChat Pay/Alipay, but carry some cash.
  • Wi-Fi: Rent a portable Wi-Fi or buy a local SIM at Guangzhou Baiyun Airport.

Now that you’re equipped with the best travel insights, pack your bags and get ready for an unforgettable Guangzhou-Zhuhai adventure!
