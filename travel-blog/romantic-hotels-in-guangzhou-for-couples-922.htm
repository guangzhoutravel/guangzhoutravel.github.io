Romantic Hotels in Guangzhou for Couples

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Guangzhou, the vibrant metropolis of Southern China, is not just a bustling business hub but also a hidden gem for couples seeking a romantic getaway. With its blend of modern skyscrapers, historic landmarks, and lush green spaces, the city offers a perfect backdrop for lovebirds. Whether you're celebrating an anniversary, planning a surprise proposal, or simply escaping the daily grind, Guangzhou’s romantic hotels provide the ideal setting. Here’s a curated list of the most enchanting stays for couples.

Luxurious Retreats with Stunning Views

The Ritz-Carlton, Guangzhou

Perched on the top floors of the Tianhe CBD, The Ritz-Carlton offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Pearl River. Couples can indulge in a private dinner at their Michelin-starred restaurant or unwind in the spa with couple’s treatments. The elegantly designed rooms feature floor-to-ceiling windows, making it a dreamy spot to watch the city lights twinkle at night.

Four Seasons Hotel Guangzhou

Located in the heart of the city, the Four Seasons combines luxury with intimacy. Their Pearl River-facing suites come with deep soaking tubs—perfect for a relaxing bath together. Don’t miss their rooftop bar, where you can sip cocktails while enjoying the skyline.

Boutique Hotels with Unique Charm

The Temple House

Nestled in the historic Liwan District, The Temple House blends contemporary design with traditional Chinese architecture. The courtyard suites offer privacy and serenity, ideal for couples who appreciate culture and tranquility. Stroll hand-in-hand through the nearby Shamian Island, a colonial-era neighborhood with cobblestone streets and charming cafés.

Zhujiang Hotel

This boutique hotel is a hidden oasis with a romantic garden and koi ponds. The rooms are adorned with delicate Chinese art, creating a poetic ambiance. It’s a short walk to Yuexiu Park, where you can enjoy a peaceful boat ride on the lake.

Budget-Friendly Yet Romantic Stays

Hotel Sweetome (Tianhe Branch)

For couples on a budget, Hotel Sweetome offers themed rooms with playful decor, from Parisian-inspired suites to cozy "love nests" with heart-shaped beds. It’s a fun and affordable option for young couples looking for a quirky experience.

Wanda Realm Guangzhou

This mid-range hotel doesn’t skimp on romance. Their "Romantic Package" includes rose petal decorations, champagne, and a late checkout—perfect for a spontaneous weekend escape.

Hotels with Exceptional Dining Experiences

The Langham, Guangzhou

Known for its afternoon tea, The Langham offers a whimsical "Tiffin at the Langham" experience. Couples can enjoy delicate pastries and fine teas in a sophisticated setting. The hotel’s riverside location also makes it a great spot for evening walks.

The Westin Guangzhou

Their "Couple’s Brunch" at the Seasonal Tastes restaurant is a must-try. With live music and a lavish spread, it’s a delightful way to spend a lazy Sunday morning together.

Hotels Near Romantic Attractions

The Garden Hotel

Situated near the Guangzhou Opera House, this hotel is perfect for couples who enjoy cultural outings. After a night of ballet or symphony, retreat to their lush garden courtyard for a nightcap.

Sofitel Guangzhou Sunrich

Located close to Canton Tower, this hotel offers easy access to one of the city’s most iconic landmarks. Book a room on a higher floor to enjoy unobstructed views of the tower’s dazzling light show.

Tips for a Perfect Romantic Stay

  • Book a river cruise: Many hotels partner with Pearl River cruise companies for private dinner cruises.
  • Timing matters: Visit during the spring (February to April) for pleasant weather and blooming flowers.
  • Surprise upgrades: Call ahead to arrange rose petals, chocolates, or a cake for your partner.

Guangzhou’s romantic hotels cater to every couple’s style, whether you’re after opulence, culture, or affordability. Pack your bags and let the city of Canton weave its magic on your love story.
