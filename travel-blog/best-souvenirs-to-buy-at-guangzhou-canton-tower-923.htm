Best Souvenirs to Buy at Guangzhou Canton Tower

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Standing tall at 600 meters, the Guangzhou Canton Tower is not just an architectural marvel but also a hub for unique souvenirs that capture the essence of Guangzhou’s vibrant culture. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a frequent traveler, picking the right memento can be overwhelming. To help you out, here’s a curated list of the best souvenirs to buy at the Canton Tower, blending tradition, modernity, and a touch of Guangzhou’s charm.

Must-Have Canton Tower Memorabilia

Miniature Replicas of the Canton Tower

No trip to the tower is complete without taking home a miniature replica. These intricately designed models come in various sizes, from keychain versions to detailed desk ornaments. They make perfect gifts for architecture enthusiasts or anyone who appreciates iconic landmarks.

LED Light-Up Souvenirs

The Canton Tower is famous for its dazzling LED light displays at night. Recreate that magic at home with LED souvenirs like light-up keychains, 3D night lamps, or even a mini LED tower that changes colors. These are especially popular among kids and tech-savvy travelers.

Traditional Guangzhou Crafts with a Modern Twist

Cantonese Embroidery (Yue Xiu)

Guangzhou is renowned for its exquisite Yue Xiu embroidery, a centuries-old craft. At the Canton Tower gift shops, you’ll find modern interpretations of this art—think embroidered silk scarves, framed miniatures, or even embroidered phone cases. These pieces are lightweight and easy to pack, making them ideal souvenirs.

Porcelain with a Skyline Design

Guangdong’s porcelain is world-famous, and the Canton Tower offers unique pieces featuring the tower’s silhouette or the Pearl River skyline. Look for tea sets, decorative plates, or small trinket boxes—each item is a beautiful blend of tradition and contemporary design.

Foodie Delights from Guangzhou

Lychee-Flavored Treats

Lychee is a symbol of Guangzhou, and you’ll find plenty of lychee-infused goodies at the tower’s souvenir shops. From lychee candies and dried fruit to lychee tea, these treats are a sweet way to remember your trip. Pro tip: Grab a box of lychee mooncakes if you’re visiting during the Mid-Autumn Festival.

Dim Sum-Themed Kitchenware

Dim sum is synonymous with Guangzhou, and what better way to celebrate it than with dim sum-themed souvenirs? Look for cute chopstick rests shaped like shrimp dumplings (har gow) or steam bun (baozi) magnets. These quirky items are perfect for food lovers.

Tech and Pop Culture Souvenirs

Canton Tower-Themed Tech Accessories

For the gadget lovers, the tower’s gift shops offer a range of tech accessories like phone cases, pop sockets, and laptop sleeves featuring the tower’s sleek design. Some even glow in the dark, mimicking the tower’s nighttime illuminations.

Limited-Edition Collectibles

If you’re into pop culture, keep an eye out for limited-edition Canton Tower merchandise. These might include collaboration items with local artists, special-edition postcards, or even tower-themed Lego sets. They’re great for collectors and make for unique conversation starters.

Practical Yet Stylish Keepsakes

Pearl River Delta Maps on Silk

A practical yet artistic souvenir, these silk maps depict the Pearl River Delta and include the Canton Tower as a centerpiece. They’re lightweight, elegant, and can be framed as wall art or used as decorative scarves.

Customized Skyline Sketches

Many vendors near the tower offer quick sketches of the Guangzhou skyline, with the Canton Tower as the focal point. You can get these personalized with your name or travel dates, making them a one-of-a-kind keepsake.

Where to Buy These Souvenirs

Most of these items can be found at the official Canton Tower gift shops located on the observation decks or at the base of the tower. For a wider selection, check out the nearby malls or the Yuexiu Market, where local artisans sell their crafts.

Whether you’re looking for a small token or a statement piece, the Canton Tower’s souvenir scene has something for everyone. Each item tells a story—a slice of Guangzhou’s past, present, and futuristic skyline. Happy shopping!
