Guangzhou’s Best Vegan Restaurants Near the Fair

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Guangzhou, a bustling metropolis in southern China, is not only a global trade hub but also a paradise for food lovers. With the Canton Fair attracting thousands of international visitors every year, the demand for diverse culinary experiences has skyrocketed. For those seeking plant-based delights, Guangzhou offers an impressive array of vegan restaurants near the fairgrounds. Whether you're a dedicated vegan, a health-conscious traveler, or simply curious about meat-free dining, these spots will leave you satisfied and energized for your next business meeting or sightseeing adventure.

Why Vegan Dining in Guangzhou?

Guangzhou’s culinary scene is traditionally dominated by Cantonese flavors, often featuring seafood, roast meats, and dim sum. However, the city has embraced the global shift toward plant-based eating with creativity and flair. Many vegan restaurants here blend traditional Chinese cooking techniques with modern, sustainable ingredients, offering dishes that are both familiar and innovative.

Proximity to the Canton Fair is another major advantage. After a long day of networking and deal-making, a nourishing vegan meal can be the perfect way to unwind. Plus, plant-based dining aligns with the growing trend of eco-conscious travel, making it a great choice for visitors who want to reduce their carbon footprint while exploring Guangzhou.

Top Vegan Restaurants Near the Canton Fair

1. Happy Veggie (素和善)

Located just a 10-minute drive from the fairgrounds, Happy Veggie is a favorite among locals and expats alike. The restaurant’s minimalist décor and serene ambiance make it an ideal spot for a relaxed meal.

Must-Try Dishes:

  • Mushroom "Peking Duck" – A vegan twist on the classic, made with marinated king oyster mushrooms wrapped in thin pancakes.
  • Lotus Root Stir-Fry – Crispy lotus root with wood ear mushrooms and a savory sauce.
  • Vegan Dim Sum Platter – A selection of steamed dumplings and buns filled with seasonal vegetables.

2. Green Olive (绿橄榄)

Green Olive takes vegan dining to the next level with its fusion of Mediterranean and Cantonese flavors. The restaurant sources organic produce from local farms, ensuring freshness in every bite.

Why It Stands Out:

  • Eco-Friendly Practices – Compostable packaging and zero-waste initiatives.
  • Signature Dish: Vegan "Fish" in Sour Soup – Made with tofu skin and seaweed for an authentic texture.
  • Dessert Highlight: Coconut Milk Mango Pudding – A refreshing end to your meal.

3. Buddha’s Delight (佛跳墙素食)

Inspired by Buddhist temple cuisine, this restaurant offers an all-you-can-eat buffet with over 50 dishes. It’s perfect for groups or those who want to sample a variety of flavors.

Buffet Highlights:

  • Mock Meat Stations – From "char siu" to "sweet and sour pork," all made from soy and wheat gluten.
  • Herbal Soups – Slow-cooked broths with medicinal herbs for a health boost.
  • DIY Hot Pot – Choose your own broth and fresh veggies for a customizable experience.

Hidden Gems for Vegan Travelers

1. Veggie Table (蔬桌)

Tucked away in a quiet alley near Pazhou, Veggie Table is a cozy café with a focus on Western-style vegan comfort food. Their avocado toast and jackfruit tacos are Instagram-worthy and delicious.

2. Loving Hut (爱心小屋)

Part of an international vegan chain, Loving Hut serves affordable and flavorful dishes. Their lemongrass tofu banh mi is a must-try for sandwich lovers.

Tips for Vegan Dining in Guangzhou

  • Language Barrier? Use translation apps or look for menus with pictures.
  • Ask About Ingredients – Some dishes may contain hidden animal products like oyster sauce.
  • Explore Local Markets – Haizhu Wet Market has a great selection of fresh produce and vegan snacks.

Whether you're in town for business or leisure, Guangzhou’s vegan scene promises a memorable culinary journey. From upscale plant-based fine dining to humble street-side cafés, there’s something for every palate near the Canton Fair.
